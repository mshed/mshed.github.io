Residential Apartment

Client : Mr.Rahim Location : Tambaram, Chennai

Residential apartment was built to two brothers of about 1200 sq.ft land area consisting of car parking, office room, store room, and two modern flat homes. The two facade elevations were designed after the completion of the structure, to create profound frontal impact at the street level. The 3 colours used to add luxury and aesthetic beauty and pergola was added in the design. The project was completed in three months.

Wedding Hall

Client : Mr.Arivalagan Location : Tiruvallur, Chennai

A wedding hall with grand floor space and parking feasibility and the total land area of about 4800 sq.ft. Unique and different, the elevation was designed to promise that people would think of it as a new modern wedlock building. Box type projections on the facade were used to add aesthetic beauty, to differentiate on this monolithic block and to reduce direct heat. The project is not yet started.
